# Privacy Policy — Lichess Chat Translator

**Last updated: June 6, 2026**

## Overview

Lichess Chat Translator is a Chrome extension that translates chat messages on lichess.org using Chrome's built-in Translator API. All translation happens locally on your device.

## Data Collection

This extension does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or chat content. No data ever leaves your device.

## How It Works

Chat text is passed directly to Chrome's built-in Translator API, which runs entirely on-device. Nothing is sent to external servers or third-party services.

## Permissions

The extension runs only on `lichess.org` pages. It does not request access to your browsing history, cookies, or any other data outside of the Lichess chat interface.
